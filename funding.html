About Lab

Our lab is interested in linking cell biology of the neuron to function and behaviour of the organism. We focus on how transport of axonal cargo is regulated, using C. elegans as a model organism. Using a combination of classical genetics, molecular & biochemical approaches and in vivo imaging techniques, our lab investigates synaptic vesicle protein trafficking, regulation of cargo-motor interactions, axoplasmic crowding and regulation of cargo distribution along the neuronal process.

Research Summary

Regulation of pre-synaptic vesicle transport.

Regulation of mitochondrial transport and distribution in neurons.

Developing new in vivo tools to help us image cargo transport in neurons.

Quantitative understanding of vesicle transport.
